FAQ

How long does shipping take? +

Shipping times vary by region. EU orders typically arrive in 5 – 8 business days, UK orders in 7 – 10 business days, and worldwide orders in 10 – 15 business days. All orders are processed within 2 – 4 business days before shipping. Visit our Delivery page for full details.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have the original tags attached. To start a return, reach out to us via our contact page and we'll send you a return label with full instructions.

What sizes do you offer? +

Our t-shirts and apparel are available in sizes XS through 3XL. We offer a unisex, relaxed fit on most styles. Each product page includes a detailed size guide with measurements in both centimeters and inches to help you find your perfect fit.

How do I track my order? +

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package in real time. If you haven't received your tracking email within 5 business days, check your spam folder or contact our support team.

Are your products sustainable? +

We're committed to reducing waste. All our products are printed on demand, meaning we only produce what's ordered — no excess inventory ends up in landfills. We use 100% organic cotton for our tees and work with print partners who prioritize eco-friendly inks and processes.

Can I cancel or modify my order? +

Since our products are made to order, we can only cancel or modify orders within the first 2 hours of placement. After that, production has likely already started. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make changes.

Do you offer wholesale? +

Yes! We offer wholesale pricing for bulk orders of 50+ items. Whether you're a retailer, event organizer, or just someone who really loves memes, reach out to us through our contact page and we'll put together a custom quote for you.

How do I contact support? +

You can reach our support team through the contact page. We typically respond within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, include "URGENT" in your subject line and we'll prioritize your request.
