Our Story

Born from memes, built for culture.

How It Started

It started the way most great things do — in a group chat. A crew of meme-obsessed friends who spent more time scrolling than sleeping had the same thought: why isn't anyone making clothes that actually match our humor?

Every brand was either too serious, too generic, or trying way too hard. We wanted tees that hit like a perfectly timed reply — the kind that makes strangers stop you on the street and say, "Where did you get that?"

So we built Pretrendy. A place where internet culture meets quality streetwear. Every design starts as something that made us laugh, think, or screenshot at 2am. Then we print it on the softest 100% cotton we can find, because looking funny should still feel good.

We're not chasing trends — we're setting them. Before the meme goes mainstream, before the format gets stale, we've already got it on a tee. That's the Pretrendy promise: trend before it trends.
