Order Processing

All orders are processed within 2 – 4 business days after payment confirmation. Our products are printed on demand, which means each item is freshly produced specifically for you.

During peak seasons or promotional periods, processing times may be slightly longer. We appreciate your patience while we craft your perfect meme tee.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package in real time through the carrier's website. If you haven't received your tracking email within 5 business days of placing your order, please check your spam folder or contact us.

Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. To be eligible for a return, the following conditions must be met:

  • Item must be unworn and unwashed
  • Original tags must be attached
  • Item must be in its original packaging

How to Return

  1. 1 Contact our support team via the contact page to initiate your return.
  2. 2 You will receive a return shipping label and instructions via email.
  3. 3 Ship the item back to us. Refunds are processed within 5 – 7 business days after we receive the return.
